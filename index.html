About Me

Ideas have always been at the heart of all inspiration for me. As a child, I built a strong reading habit to whet my appetite for new ideas. Over the years, I realized that ideas and data are inseparable and learnt to quantify ideas with data. This gave me a keen eye for detail and nurtured strong articulation skills.

My segue into Analytics began with Mu Sigma, a data science company where I worked for 3 years. Having closely worked with experts in the line of analytics has helped me understand how businesses are adapting to the new data wave and the broad range of analytical functions applicable to online marketing. I find the profound impact that data can create and how it can drive changes in economy, governance and society to be truly exciting.

A rich, vibrant and highly competitive ecosystem at UT helped me sharpen my skills at solving problems that lie at the confluence of Business, Math and Technology during my master's degree. I am currently working at Amazon in the Payment Risk space.
